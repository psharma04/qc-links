Resources and forms for the Queer Space.
The Queer Space is located on Level 9 of the Chemical Sciences Building (F10), and is accessible to all QueerCo members from 8am to 6pm on weekdays during the Academic Term (excluding Summer Term).
To gain access to the Queer Space, please use the QueerCo Registration Form.
